Check when a caution or conviction is spent

Use this service to check when a caution or conviction becomes spent in England and Wales.

You can only check one caution or conviction at a time.

To use this service you’ll need to know:

  • the type of caution or conviction you were given
  • the date you were cautioned or convicted

If you don’t have this information you can read guidance on when cautions and convictions become spent.
