Check when a caution or conviction is spent

Use this service to check when cautions or convictions become spent in England and Wales.

How this service works

You’ll need to know:

  • which types of caution or conviction you’ve been given
  • the dates you were sentenced
  • how long any sentences lasted

The service will give you guidance on:

  • when each of your cautions or convictions become spent
  • when to tell people, such as employers, about your criminal record

You can use the service to check convictions that include more than one sentence – for example, a single motoring conviction that includes a fine, penalty points and an endorsement.

If you do not have all the information you need to use the service, you can read general information on guidance on when cautions and convictions become spent.

You cannot use this service if you were released on bail during a conviction.
