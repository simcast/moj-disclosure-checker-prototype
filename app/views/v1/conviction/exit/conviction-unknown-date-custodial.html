Your conviction will expire 2 years after the sentence ends

After this date you will not need to declare this conviction unless you’re applying for a job that needs a full criminal record check.

Example

If your sentence ended on 17 September 2018, it would expire on 17 September 2020.

You would not need to tell people about the conviction after 17 September 2020.

About the expiry date

The expiry date of your caution is based on:

  • the information you’ve provided
  • the current law on when cautions and convictions expire
