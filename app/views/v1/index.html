Check if you need to tell people about your criminal record

Use this service to check if you need to tell anyone about a caution or conviction on your criminal record.

You might be asked about your criminal record when applying for:

  • jobs
  • college or university
  • insurance

Most cautions and convictions expire (become 'spent') after a certain length of time. You don't usually need to tell anyone about them after they become spent.

An employer might need to know about spent cautions or convictions when you apply for certain types of job.

This service is for people in England and Wales.
