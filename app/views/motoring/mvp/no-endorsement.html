Sorry, we cannot tell you the exact date your conviction becomes spent

This service is currently in beta. At the moment it can only tell you when motoring convictions with endorsements become spent.

The following guidance will help you work out if your conviction is spent.

If you were given penalty points without an endorsement

Penalty points are spent 3 years after the date the penalty points were given.

If you were disqualified without an endorsement

Disqualifications are spent on the day the disqualification ends.

If you were not given an end date for the disqualification, it will become spent 2 years after the date you were disqualified.
