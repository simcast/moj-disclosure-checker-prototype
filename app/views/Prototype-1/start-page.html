When to disclose cautions or convictions

Use this service to check when cautions or convictions become spent in England and Wales.

A spent caution or conviction is something you do not need to tell people about if they’re doing a basic criminal record check.

You’ll be given information on:

  • when each of your cautions or convictions become spent
  • when to tell people, such as employers, about your criminal record

How this service works

You’ll be given accurate results if you know:

  • the types of cautions or convictions you were given
  • the dates you were given them
  • how long any sentences lasted

If you do not have all this information, you can either:

  • enter approximate dates into service (meaning your results will also be approximate)
  • read general guidance on when cautions and convictions become spent
